Welcome to Vastlandic Aerial

Need to get a quick look at your roof or other elevated surface out of reach? Or a set of aerial photos of your property or outdoor event? ... but don't know who to call?

I'm Nick Charpentier, a Transport Canada certified Drone Operator and full-time GIS Technician based in Saskatoon, SK, Canada, looking to assist you in your small-scale jobs from a bird's-eye-view, delivering you the clear and detailed view that only an eye-in-the-sky can provide!

Please reach out to me via the Contact or Booking pages to get in touch. I look forward to discussing how I can safely and effectively assist you!

Current Services include:

  • Aerial Video & Photography:
    • Exterior Captures (Residential & Commercial Properties within 100km of Saskatoon)
    • Rooftop / Elevated Surface Imaging (FYI only, not as a Professional Inspection)
    • Weekly Photo & Video Progress Updates of Residential / Commercial Development
  • Custom Aerial Projects:
    • Special Events (Sports & Community Events)
    • Promotional Film (Small Business & Corporate Marketing Campaigns)
    • Film Industry (Documentaries & Movies)
